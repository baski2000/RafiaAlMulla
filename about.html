Family background profile


Al Mulla Family has its respectable presence and pioneering role in the development of trade and business life in Dubai rooted back in the early thirties of the last century. For decades, this prominent family was a major player in the development of Dubai as a commercial city by establishing the first “fundamental” economic activities, such as the first bank (Dubai National Bank), the first power plant (Dubai Electric Company), the Dubai Telephone Company, the first insurance provider (Dubai Insurance Company), etc. HE Mohammed Saeed Al Mulla as chairman Al Mulla group of companies owns various landmarks in Dubai such as The American Hospital in Dubai, The Ritz Carlton Hotel in Dubai among other prestigious holdings including:

  • Al Mulla Construction Company(ACC)one of the oldest and largest contractor in this region.
  • Abjar Hotels – Hotel’s portfolio of the Group which includes the Ritz-Carlton, Sheraton Dubai Hotel & Towers, Renaissance Hotel, Dubai and Ramada Hotel, Dubai.
  • American Hospital Dubai – the most sophisticated health care facility operating according to American Standard and Certifications operating from Dubai with 100 beds and over 45 doctors.
  • Al Mulla Plaza – Shopping and Residential complex and also other real estates in Dubai’s prime and prestigious locations.
  • Al Mulla Jewelery – having exclusive agency for Breitling Watches and a chain of seven Jewelery shops within the Emirates under KNOZ brand.
  • The Group is instrumental in establishing first wave public companies in Dubai, as such it has major shareholding in blue chip companies as National Bank of Dubai, Oman Refreshments Company (Pepsicola Bottling) and Dubai Insurance.
